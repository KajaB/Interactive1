The Evolution of Scrollbars

What are Scrollbars?

Scrollbars are the vertical bar on the right side of a window or a horizontal bar at the bottom of a window
that is used to move the window contents up and down or left and right.

The sole purpose of a standard scroll bar is to enable the user to generate scrolling requests for viewing the entire content of the client area.
